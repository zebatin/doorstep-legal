Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Doorstep ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the Doorstep mobile application ("App"). By using the App you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

Short version: We collect only what we need to run the app. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted — we cannot read them. We do not sell your data.

1. Who We Are

Doorstep is a neighbourhood-based social and dating app. The service is operated from Australia. For privacy enquiries, contact us at: privacy@doorstep.app

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account details — email address and password (stored via Supabase Auth)
  • Profile information — display name, age, suburb (location_city), gender, intent, bio, and up to six photos
  • Messages — text messages sent between matched users (end-to-end encrypted; see Section 5)
  • Suburb board posts and comments — public posts, reactions, poll votes, and RSVP responses within your suburb

Information collected automatically

  • Push notification token — a device token used to deliver notifications (stored against your profile; never shared with third parties for marketing)
  • Usage data — swipe actions (knocks, rings, waves) stored to power the matching system
  • App interactions — match creation, message timestamps, and read receipts

Information we do NOT collect

  • Precise GPS location — we use only your manually entered suburb name
  • Contacts, microphone, or camera roll beyond photos you explicitly upload
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking pixels

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show your profile to other users in your suburb and facilitate connections
  • To deliver push notifications for messages, matches, and suburb activity
  • To enforce daily/weekly usage limits based on your plan (Free, Pro, Max)
  • To process subscription payments via Apple In-App Purchases
  • To detect and prevent abuse, spam, and policy violations
  • To improve the App based on aggregate (non-identifying) usage patterns

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information on the following bases:

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — safety, fraud prevention, and service improvement
  • Consent — where you have explicitly agreed (e.g. push notification permissions)

5. End-to-End Encryption

All messages between matched users are encrypted on your device before being transmitted and stored. We use X25519 key exchange and XSalsa20-Poly1305 encryption (via TweetNaCl). Your private encryption key never leaves your device. This means we cannot read your messages, and neither can anyone else without physical access to your device.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Data is stored in their secure cloud infrastructure. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Expo (Push Notifications) — push notification tokens are transmitted through Expo's push service to deliver alerts. See Expo Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. RevenueCat receives your app user ID (a random UUID) and purchase receipt data. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Apple — In-App Purchase payments are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive your payment card details.
  • Law enforcement — if required by law or to protect the safety of users.

7. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained while your account is active
  • Messages are retained to support the conversation experience; deleting your account removes your messages
  • Suburb board posts are retained unless you delete them or your account is removed
  • Aggregate/anonymised analytics may be retained indefinitely

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — update your profile information at any time within the App
  • Deletion — delete your account via Settings → Delete Account. This removes your profile, photos, and messages permanently within 30 days
  • Portability — request an export of your data by emailing us
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any right, email privacy@doorstep.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Doorstep is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage, end-to-end message encryption, and access controls on our database. No system is perfectly secure; in the event of a data breach we will notify affected users as required by law.

11. Australian Privacy Act

We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles. If you have a complaint about how we handle your personal information, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we make material changes we will notify you via a push notification or in-app message. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
